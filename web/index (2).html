Common Misconceptions

I'm too late! You have to start very early for second language learning or you will miss the boat
Although young children may seem to pick up language quickly, older children can also master second languages, all that matters is time and effort put into learning the language.
Will my bilingual child mix up languages?
Mixing languages is temporary and goes away as the child's language vocabulary increases
Only native speakers and teachers can teach children second languages?
As long as children are getting exposure to the language and are interacting with adults, the child will be able to pick up the language and use it effectively.
Two languages are the most a very young child should be exposed to
The more the merrier, if the child has an opportunity to engage in meaningful interaction in each language on a daily basis, THEN GO FOR IT!

Benefits of Bilingualism

Cognitive Development

  • Children who are bilingual have gain skilled literacy skills
  • Children who are bilingual are much more focused on tasks, which will helps in being successful in school
  • Children who are bilingual are better at thinking outside of the box and coming up with multiple solutions when they are faced with a problem than those who are monolingual
  • Children who are bilingual tend to score better on test than monolingual children.

Health and Social

  • Children who are bilingual have been said to have reduced levels of anxiety
  • Children who are bilingual maintain strong ties with their family, culture and community.
  • Children who are bilingual have reduced levels of loneliness and have higher self-esteem
  • Children who are bilingual translate which allows the child to practice the language through verbal interaction

Global

  • Children who are bilingual learn and are more accepting of others from different cultures.
  • Children who are bilingual have a higher and better chances of getting high paying jobs in the future
  • Children who are bilingual are more open to participating in community activities.
